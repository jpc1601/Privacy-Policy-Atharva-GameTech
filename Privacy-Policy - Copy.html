Privacy Policy - Atharva GameTech

Last updated:24th April, 2022

Your privacy is important to Atharva GameTech (for all published apps) and We are committed to protect your data and privacy. Please have a look at the privacy policy.

Our Privacy Policy explains how your personal data is collected and processed when you install and use any of our mobile application/game published by "Atharva GameTech" developer from the Google Play store.

This Privacy Policy complies with GDPR in the EU and COPPA in the US.

If you are not agree with our policy, please do not use our games/apps.

This Privacy Policy is updated on a regular basis. If we make changes, You will get to know by checking the date at the top of the policy. You will be deemed to have accepted these changes if you continue to use the Service.

========================================================================

Collection of Information:

We do not collect any Personal/sensitive Information about you, such as your full name, residential/office address, email address, contact number, photos, precise geolocation information, banking information, or user names.

Our apps may only collect Unique Device Identifier("Google Advertising ID") as personal information. We may collect certain non-personal information about you, which is information that does not identify you and cannot be used to contact you directly.

List of Non-personal information may be collected:

  • your age, non-precise geolocation information, gender and so on
  • your device's hardware/software/firmware, screen resolution and so on
  • your app usage, progress in the game, scores, preferred options, and so on
  • We have no control over the type of information you sent or the level of privacy of that information if you send it through our website or email directly.
========================================================================

For more information on how we store and process Directly-sent Information, see the sections below:

Personal Information:

  • We utilise personal information to display appropriate contextual adverts in the app to children (as defined by GDPR in the EU, COPPA in the US, and comparable applicable legislation in other jurisdictions).
  • Non-child users who have granted us permission to share their advertising IDs with our ad network partners (as further defined under Sharing of Information) for the purpose of delivering them with personalised advertisements in the app (behavioural advertising / targeted advertising) are provided with personal information.
Non-personal Information:
  • We utilise to track and analyse general app distribution statistics such as audience, country, devices, installs, and sessions, and then maintain and enhance the app based on that data.

Directly-sent information:

  • As previously stated, we can obtain unrestricted data and information from users via email.
  • We usually disregard and do not process any Personal Information obtained through such requests, instead analysing general opinions, complaints, and recommendations in order to improve the quality of our applications. However, in exceptional circumstances, such as support inquiries, unintended purchases, personal data removal requests, or any other type of complaint about our applications and services, we may need to use required Personal Information to address an issue and satisfy the user.

Sharing of Information:

Only the following recipients receive information about you:

  • In order to offer relevant advertisements, Google AdMob and Unity, an advertising network firm, collaborated. We give an advertising network firm your advertising ID so that they can serve you personalized adverts (targeted ads) within the app.
  • Please visit Google AdMob's Privacy Policy for more information.
  • If you are from the European Union (EU), we only disclose your advertising ID if you have given your approval, as required by the GDPR.

Opt-out from Ads Personalization:

  • Turn off Ads Personalization from Google Ad Settings. For more information please click here.
  • Uninstall the app from your device.

The service we use to collect and analyse statistics are Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics. We only share non-personal information with you, such as information about how you use the app. For more information please click here.

Children's Privacy:

Some of our apps are specifically designed for children, as stated in the Google Play store's publishing information. We do not collect, handle, or disclose any Personal Information about users in these apps, including advertising ID.

We anticipate that children will not install or access our non-child-directed apps unless they are under close parental supervision. As a result, we do not collect, process, or disclose Personal Information on children without parental agreement. If you are from the European Economic Area, we will ask for your authorization to use and disclose your advertising ID, which is your personal information, in compliance with the GDPR.

Security:

We care about the security of your Personal Information, but keep in mind that no method of Internet transmission or computer storage is completely safe. While we make every effort to protect your Personal Information using commercially reasonable techniques, we cannot guarantee its total security.
